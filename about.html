How IntelCRM Started

IntelCRM was born from frustration. We watched talented Kenyan sales reps lose winnable deals because they walked into calls unprepared. Not because they weren't smart — but because prep was manual, time-consuming, and inconsistent.

Traditional CRMs didn't solve this. They tracked deals after calls happened. They logged activities. They showed pipelines. But they didn't arm reps before battle.

So we built IntelCRM: a tactical command center that generates AI-powered battle cards in minutes. It combines BANT, MEDDIC, SPIN, and Challenger methodologies into one readiness score. It gives reps opening questions, objection responses, and next steps — before they dial.

We're not replacing your CRM. We're the intelligence layer before your CRM. The war room before the battlefield. The prep that turns good reps into unstoppable closers.
